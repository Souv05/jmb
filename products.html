Applications of our Microsilica


Microsilica, or silica fume, is widely used in the construction industry due to its beneficial properties. Here are the key applications:



High-Performance Concrete (HPC)


  • Infrastructure Projects: Enhances strength and durability for bridges, tunnels, and highways.
  • Pavements: Improves wear resistance in high-traffic areas.

Ultra-High-Performance Concrete (UHPC)


  • Architectural Elements: Allows for lightweight, high-strength panels.
  • Precast Products: Used in beams and slabs requiring exceptional load capacity.

Durability Enhancement


  • Reduced Permeability: Minimizes water and chemical ingress for structures in harsh environments.

Applications of our Biomass Pellets

Biomass pellets are small, compressed pellets made from organic materials. Here are the key applications:



Energy Production

  • Heating: Used in residential and industrial boilers for a renewable heating alternative.
  • Electricity Generation: Burned in power plants to generate electricity, contributing to a cleaner energy mix.

Industrial Uses


  • Manufacturing: Utilized in the production of particleboard, MDF, and other wood-based products.
  • Cooking Fuels: Serve as cooking fuel in stoves and ovens in some regions.

Agriculture

  • Animal Bedding: Used as bedding for livestock, providing comfort and managing waste.
  • Soil Amendments: Applied as organic fertilizers or soil conditioners to enhance soil quality.

Environmental Benefits


  • Carbon Neutral: Considered carbon-neutral as the CO2 released during combustion is offset by the CO2 absorbed during biomass growth.
  • Waste Reduction: Helps reduce agricultural and forestry waste and promotes sustainable waste management.

Transportation Fuel


  • Biofuels: Converted into biofuels for transportation, helping reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

Applications of Rice Husk Ash


Protective Coating

Used as a tarnish covering layer to prevent oxidation of molten metal during steel production.


Thermal Insulation

Provides excellent insulation properties, reducing heat loss from molten steel.


Refractory Material

Acts as a cost-effective and environmentally friendly alternative to conventional refractory materials.


Reduction of Surface Oxidation

Minimizes the formation of scale on the surface of hot steel products.


Improved Surface Finish

Helps achieve a smoother surface on steel products by preventing surface defects caused by oxidation.


Cost Efficiency

Offers a low-cost solution compared to other tarnish covering compounds.


Sustainable and Eco-friendly

Utilizes an agricultural byproduct, contributing to waste minimization and sustainable practices in the steel industry.


Ease of Application

Can be applied in powdered form or as part of a slurry, ensuring adaptability in existing plant processes.
